Computer-Based Testing System

A computer-based testing software (CBT) built in partial fulfilment of the ALX-Holberton Software Enginering Program.
While the paper-based test involves a long, tedious process of conducting exams and evaluating results, the computer-based test delivers fast, reliable and error-free assessment solutions.

STUDENTS

Sudents can login in with their matriculation number (student ID) and password, view available exams, take exams that are timed and see the results of the exams they took.

EXAMINERS

Examiners can also login with their username and password, manage exams by adding and editing exam questions, they can also view students results through the gradebook that reecords their results according to courses taken.

ADMIN

The administrator is responsible for managing the software, the admin can manage users, both students and examiners as well as manage courses.
